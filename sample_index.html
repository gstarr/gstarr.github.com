Hi! I'm Graham Starr.

I'm a junior at Tufts University, pursuing a double major in International Relations and Physics, with a minor in Linguistics. My focus is on innovation, specifically in the cross-sectionality of interdisciplinary fields, and the ways in which we can apply science and technology to international affairs. I have experience with design, web development, journalism, public speaking and speechwriting, and public relations, among other things, and hope to combine them in some capacity in the future. I am currently the General Manager of WMFO Medford, Tufts Freeform Radio, the Public Relations Director and Webmaster for the Alliance Linking Leaders in Education and the Services (ALLIES), a national civil-military relations group, and the Managing Editor for The Zamboni, Tufts' Only Intentionally Funny Publication. In my spare time, you can see me performing stand-up comedy, learning a new language, doing something musical, or reading.
