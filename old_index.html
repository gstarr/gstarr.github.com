Hi! I'm Graham Starr.

I'm a junior at Tufts University, pursuing a double major in International Relations and Physics, with a minor in Linguistics. My focus is on innovation theory, specifically its effect in the cross-section of interdisciplinary fields, and the ways in which we can apply science and technology to international affairs most effectively. I have experience with design, web development, journalism, speechwriting, and public relations, among other things, and hope to combine them in some capacity in the future.

I am currently the General Manager of WMFO Medford, Tufts Freeform Radio, the Public Relations Director and Webmaster for the Alliance Linking Leaders in Education and the Services (ALLIES), a national civil-military relations group, and the Managing Editor for The Zamboni, Tufts' Only Intentionally Funny Publication. If I have any free time, you can find me performing stand-up comedy, learning a new skill, hiking, rock climbing, or reading. You can find me on Twitter @GrahamStarr, check out my Portfolio, see what cool Projects I'm working on, or find other ways to Contact Me. Feel free to also check out my resume here!
