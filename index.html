Hi!

I'm a writer, designer, editor, and data nerd. People sometimes pay me to “analyze” things and I write a lot of articles that I always forget to add to my portfolio.

I enjoy doing "serious" things like data analysis, and have been involved in roles with titles that have words like "research" and "intern" in the title. I also do less serious things like comedy, and I write long-form articles and screenplays and run-on sentences where everything is in "quotes" and musicals and sometimes I build and design things with computers.

I'm currently the Editor-in-Chief of The Zamboni, Tufts University's oldest student-run humor and satire magazine. I was previously Managing Editor, where I redesigned the magazine's layout and print organization, and ran Art Direction and web presence. If I have any free time, you can find me writing, designing, learning a new skill, making lists, rock climbing, or reading.
