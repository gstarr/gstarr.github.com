Hi! I'm Graham Starr.

I'm a rising senior at Tufts University, pursuing a double major in International Relations and Physics, with a minor in Linguistics. My interests have concerned innovation theory, specifically its effect in the cross-section of interdisciplinary fields, and the ways in which we can apply science and technology to international affairs most effectively. I have experience with design, journalism, marketing, operational strategy, and growth hacking, among other things, and hope to combine them in some capacity in the future.

I'm currently the Editor-in-Chief of The Zamboni, a student-run humor and satire magazine founded in 1989. I was previously Managing Editor, where I redesigned the magazine's layout and print organization, and ran Art Direction and web presence. If I have any free time, you can find me writing, designing, learning a new skill, rock climbing, or reading.
