Hi! I'm Graham Starr.

I'm a junior at Tufts University, pursuing a double major in International Relations and Physics, with a minor in Linguistics. My focus is on innovation theory, specifically its effect in the cross-section of interdisciplinary fields, and the ways in which we can apply science and technology to international affairs most effectively. I'm currently the Editor-in-Chief for The Zamboni, Tufts University's "Only Intentionally Funny Magazine," where I also run Art Direction and web presence. I spent the past year as Managing Editor, re-designing the magazine's layout and print organization.

If I have any free time, you can find me writing, designing, learning a new skill, rock climbing, or reading.
