Graham Starr
Hi! I'm Graham Starr.

I'm a writer, designer, editor, and data nerd. I'm usually employed to “analyze” things and I write a lot of articles that I always forget to add to my portfolio.

I do serious things like data and research analysis, and have been involved in roles with titles that have words like "cyber" and "marketing" and "research" and "intern" in the title. I also do less serious things like comedy and I write long-form articles and screenplays and run-on sentences and musicals and sometimes I build and design things with computers.

I'm currently the Editor-in-Chief of The Zamboni, Tufts University's oldest student-run humor and satire magazine. I was previously Managing Editor, where I redesigned the magazine's layout and print organization, and ran Art Direction and web presence. If I have any free time, you can find me writing, designing, learning a new skill, making lists, rock climbing, or reading.

Feel free to connect with me on Twitter or LinkedIn, or see what I'm listening to on Last.fm. If you want, you can check out my resume here or send me an email!
